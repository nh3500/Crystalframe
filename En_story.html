INVENTION STORY

2015

First time to be in States, I met PETER LIK gallery, there were many  panoramic pictures enchant me. The most impressive one is ACRYLIC PRINT. Which beauty inspired me to devote into the invention of crystalframe.


2016

Hundred times of drawing and design, I brought the initial product to join NEW YORK ARTEXPO . At that time, although it is not perfect enough, still draw some people’s attention. And this proves the idea is possible. Then it continues for another three years development.


2018

We brought the new version of crystalframe again to be at NEW YORK ARTEXPO. This time we gained the variety of applause and compliments from people, and we decided to apply for international patents.


2019

We have started the business in USA , Taiwan and Japan and gained the patents certificates at the same time . crystalframe is more a spirit of invention that makes impossible possible.

To recreate the frames from tradition to be innovation. Stylish of western of acrylic prints. Simple framing DIY and patented. Fit in offices, houses and public. Whether it is for art decoration or advertisement, it makes the artwork 3D effect. Suction system strengthen the print goes flat and more quality. Frameless makes the pictures to be possible decorated as an huge modular art, which also keeps the original tastes. Floating crystalframe makes a perfect balance style as acrylic print. Elegant, practical and environmental.
